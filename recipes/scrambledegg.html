Scrambled Egg

Description

Protein, pragmatism, party.

Ingredients

  • 1x egg
  • 1x teaspoon of butter

Steps

  1. Crack your egg and empty the contents into a mixing bowl or glass.
  2. Using a fork, whisk your egg vigorously until it becomes one with itself.
  3. Preheat your pan to medium heat.
  4. Add the butter to the pan.
  5. Once the butter has melted and is brown, pour your egg into the pan.
  6. Mix the egg continously to cook as much of the surface area as possible.
  7. After around 5 minutes, your egg should be cooked, indicated by a slightly brown colour.
  8. Dish up the egg.
  9. Nice.
