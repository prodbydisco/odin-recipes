Cheese on Toast

Description

Cheese on toast is a very quick and easy dish that tastes great. Got bread? Got cheese? Got heat? Let's go!

Ingredients

  • 1x slice of bread
  • 1x slice of cheese
  • sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Get your bread to room temperature.
  2. Slice enough cheese to cover one side of the bread but do not keep the cheese applied, move it to one side.
  3. Place enough foil to host your bread on the oven rack and preheat to 180 degrees celsius.
  4. Then, place your bread on the foil and let it cook until the top side is golden brown.
  5. Next, flip the bread over and apply the cheese to the un-toasted side.
  6. Leave it cook for another few minutes until the cheese starts to lightly bubble (watch carefully).
  7. Finally remove your cheese on toast from the oven and leave it cool off for a couple of minutes before eating.
